# GTM Systems & Process Playbooks

This directory contains a collection of playbooks outlining the core systems and processes a Go-to-Market Engineer (GTME) should be familiar with. These are living documents that describe our GTM motions and the thinking behind them.

## Core Concepts

A GTME's role is to be the technical bridge between the product and the market. This involves deeply understanding the customer, the product, and the data that connects them. These playbooks are designed to codify the best practices for doing so.

## Playbook Index

### Lead & Pipeline Management
*   **Lead Prioritization & Scoring:** A framework for identifying and focusing on the highest potential leads.
*   **Pipeline Velocity Analysis:** How to measure and improve the speed at which leads move through the sales funnel.
*   **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) Definition:** A data-driven approach to defining and refining your target customer.
*   **Total Addressable Market (TAM) Mapping:** A methodology for mapping and sizing the total market opportunity.

### Sales & Revenue Operations
*   **Automated Prospecting & First Touch:** A system for identifying and engaging with prospects in a personalized, scalable way.
*   **Sales Demo Framework:** A structured approach to delivering compelling and effective product demonstrations.
*   **CRM System Management:** Best practices for leveraging and maintaining the CRM system.
*   **The Offer:** Crafting and iterating on the core value proposition and offer.

### Customer & Market Intelligence
*   **Competitive Analysis:** A process for tracking and analyzing competitors.
*   **Market Segmentation:** How to divide the market into approachable segments.

### Foundational Methodologies
*   **The Scientific Method in GTM:** Applying experimentation and data analysis to GTM strategies.
